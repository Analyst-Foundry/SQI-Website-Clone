Available Options in the PRE-ND Programmes

Computer Science

This programme provides students with a strong foundation in mathematics, basic computing concepts, and digital workplace proficiency, preparing them for National Diploma studies in computer science and related ICT fields.

Accountancy (With ICT)

Designed to prepare students for ND studies, this programme combines the foundational accounting knowledge with essential ICT skills and core subjects such as mathematics, commerce, and economics.

Business Administration (With ICT)

This programme introduces core business and communication concepts alongside ICT-based learning, equipping students with the academic foundation required for National Diploma studies in business disciplines.

2026/2027 Admission Into Pre-National Diploma (PRE-ND) Programmes

Applications are hereby invited from suitably qualified candidates for admission into the Pre-National Diploma (Pre-ND) Programmes of SQI College of ICT for the 2026 Academic Year. The Pre-ND Programme is a structured preparatory pathway designed to support secondary school graduates who require additional academic grounding before progressing into National Diploma (ND) studies.

The programme focuses on strengthening students' core academic subjects, improving technical competence, and developing essential digital and learning skills required for success in a tertiary academic environment. Through guided instruction, academic support, and ICT-based learning, candidates are adequately prepared for a smooth and successful transition into National Diploma programmes upon meeting the required progression standards.

Method of Application

The application window opens on January 5, 2026.
  • Interested candidates are to obtain the Pre-ND application form at the cost of ₦10,000 at sqi.edu.ng/admission.
  • Payment Method is via the application portal.
  • Successful candidates are to pay an acceptance fee of ₦20,000.
  • After payment, candidates are required to forward proof of payment to the Admissions Office via 0906 281 9993. (The resumption is on February 23, 2026)

Benefits of the Programme

  • Equip students with the foundational knowledge required for the National Diploma Program.
  • Candidates receive priority admission on the merit list.
  • Improve candidates' academic performance, especially in core subjects.
  • Prepare candidates for the next JAMB and O-Level examination.
  • Access to digital workspace proficiency and early exposure to ICT-based skills.
  • Understand and settle into higher-level academic settings.
  • Prepare candidates for ND-admission, examinations, and assessments.
  • Develop candidates' soft skills for effective performance and growth in the modern tech world.
