SQI College of ICT at a Glance

Founded in 2008, SQI (Soft Quest Incorporated) College of ICT is an NBTE accredited institution in Nigeria, offering Professional, National Diploma, and National Innovation Diploma in high-demand Software Engineering, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, and Product Design e.t.c.

SQI's hands-on training model ensures graduates complete real-world projects and are industry-ready from day one.

SQI has campuses across Nigeria, including: Ogbomoso Campus, Dugbe (Ibadan) Campus, Iwo Road (Ibadan) Campus, Challenge (Ibadan) Campus, Abeokuta Campus, Lagos Campus, Osogbo Campus, Abuja Campus and Virtual Campus.

Making Africa a Pool of Tech Talents

Students at SQI College of ICT learn through immersive, project-based training supported by experienced faculty, modern facilities, and a strong mentorship culture that promotes inclusion and productivity.

Our programs are designed to meet global industry standards, combining quality instruction with continuous research and feedback from industry partners to ensure graduates are truly job-ready.

As a private technical institution, we provide a credible alternative pathway to higher education through competency-based training for youths and working adults. In collaboration with government and industry, we help address skills gaps, expand access to technical education, and contribute to workforce development that supports national and global economic growth.

Our Target Groups
We provide credible alternative forms of education that will cater to the needs of:

  • i. School leavers who wish to acquire demonstrable practical skills to secure employment or generate employment
  • ii. Persons seeking career paths that do not need university degrees
  • iii. Persons who have not studied for some time and desire to do so
  • iv. Persons without time for full-time study but want to enhance their skills
  • v. Persons wishing to go into self-employment
  • vi. University graduates seeking employable skills and
  • vii. Adults seeking opportunities to re-skill themselves.

Objectives and Scope
Our objective is to expand access to vocational and technical education by providing industry-relevant, competency-based ICT skills that serve as a credible alternative to traditional higher education.

We also envisage to fulfill the following specific objectives:

  1. Expand access to post-basic education for learners of all ages
  2. Provide flexible, private sector–driven training aligned with industry needs
  3. Bridge the gap between education, technology, and the labor market
  4. Deliver modern, up-to-date curricula using 21st-century technologies
  5. Develop creative, critical thinkers capable of transforming skills into value
  6. Improve employability and promote economic empowerment
  7. Economically empower the individual and community by increasing employability
  8. Set new standards in design and delivery of competency and skills based Education and Training
  9. Provide industry expertise, resources, and practical know-how through training and partnerships
  10. Create pathways to employment through strong industry partnerships

Making Africa a Tech Continent… One Mind at a Time!

SQI College of ICT envisions a future where Africa leads in technology innovation and digital transformation. We believe that through quality, accessible, and practical ICT education, Africa can produce world-class technology talents who compete globally.

Our vision is to be the premier technology college in Africa — recognized for excellence in training, industry partnerships, and producing graduates who are not just job-ready but capable of creating jobs and driving innovation across the continent.

We see a Nigeria — and an Africa — where skills-based education is respected and valued on par with traditional university degrees; where young people have multiple credible pathways to economic empowerment; and where technology becomes the engine of sustainable development and national growth.

To achieve this vision, we continuously invest in our faculty, infrastructure, curriculum, and partnerships — building an institution that is student-centered, industry-aligned, and future-focused.

What We Stand For

We are committed to:

  • Quality, competency-based training aligned with industry needs
  • Flexible learning structures that support students and working professionals
  • Bridging the gap between education and employment through strong industry collaboration

As part of national efforts to expand access to technical and vocational education, we provide a credible alternative pathway to higher education—equipping youths and adults with practical, employable skills required in today's economy.
