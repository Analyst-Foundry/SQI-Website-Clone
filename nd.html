Why Choose SQI College of ICT?

At SQI College of ICT, we are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of tech leaders. Our National Diploma (ND) programs are designed to provide students with the practical skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Requirements

Eligibility

Here are the processes to gain admission to the college's National Diploma program?

1

Five (5) Credit Pass at your O'Level (WAEC/NECO/GCE/NABTEB) result is required in relevant subjects not more than 2 sittings. English Language and Mathematics are COMPULSORY.

2

Apply through JAMB and choose "SQI College of ICT" as your first choice.

3

A minimum JAMB score of 120 is required in your UTME.

4

Obtain the Application/Post UTME form at the college or online: Click here

5

Attend a scheduled screening (exam and interview) at the college's main campus.

6

Check your email regularly for admission updates.

Who Can Apply?
Whether you are a student of Arts, Commercial, or Science, you are welcome to apply. Our programs are open to all disciplines!
Additionally, applicants with Awaiting Results can also apply. Just ensure all required documents are completed before the end of your program.
