Programme 01

NATIONAL DIPLOMA PROGRAMME

Please, read carefully all the instructions documented in this guide.

Eligibility

  • Candidates must chose "SQI COLLEGE OF ICT" as their most preferred (1st) Choice Institution on their JAMB portal and scored 100 and above are eligible to apply for the Post UTME Screening.
  • Also, candidates who did not choose the "SQI COLLEGE OF ICT" and are willing to change to the Institution as their most preferred (1st) choice are also advised to apply for the Post UTME Screening.
  • All applicants are required to have a minimum of five (5) credits in either WAEC, NECO or GCE O' Level, which must include English Language, Mathematics and any other three (3) relevant subjects as may be specified by each department.
  • The five (5) O' Level credits should be obtained in not more than two (2) sittings.

Programme 02

PROFESSIONAL DIPLOMA

Eligibility

Anyone is eligible to apply for the professional certificate program at SQI College ICT, be it a young school leaver, an undergraduate, an employed graduate, an unemployed graduate or a professional. It's open to anyone and everyone, provided you have the passion to learn.

Requirements

There are no special requirements to get enrolled in the professional certificate program.

Mode of Application

The mode of application is seamless and stress-free. It's as simple as: register, make payment, and begin lectures.

NB: Payment includes a one-time registration fee of ₦10,000 and tuition fee, based on the course you want to learn. You can check out our courses here.
